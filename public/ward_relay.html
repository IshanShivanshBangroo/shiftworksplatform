Game board

Ward Relay

Each round gives you a stack of signals. Classify each as Now, Next, or Noise, choose one round action, and then resolve the board. Correct classification boosts safety and clarity. False urgency and neglected recovery increase Static and erode the next round.

Designed by Ishan Bangroo for adult audiences who want a more serious planning challenge.

Now

Act this round. Delay is costly.

Next

Important, but not this second.

Noise

Duplicated, vague, or low-value chatter.
