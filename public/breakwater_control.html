Breakwater Watch

Keep the harbor calm for 4 short rounds. Move the 3 sliders so one signal docks now, one waits outside, and one is ignored.

1. Read the three signal cards
Each card tells you what just reached the harbor.
2. Drag each slider
Pick Ignore, Wait outside, or Dock now.
3. Check the round
You get the best score when you do not treat everything like an emergency.
Worked example
A medical transfer should Dock now. A supply boat can Wait outside. A repeat radio ping can be Ignored.

Signal cards

Each round gives you three signals. Use the sliders to show what happens next. Keep it simple: one now, one wait, one noise.

What this game helps practice
Picking what needs action now. Letting safe things wait. Not giving noise the same power as a real need.
When you score well
You keep the harbor calm, backlog low, and trust steady. You do that by resisting the urge to dock everything at once.
